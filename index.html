Who i am

I'm a Web Developer based in Southern California. I graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a degree in Public Health. I first discovered web development during a course I took in college. It is pretty amazing how you can create an application from the ground up with a few key strokes and a logical thought process.

My coding knowledge began to expand significantly when I attempted to solve puzzle-like programming challenges. There were problems where they were easy to breeze through, but many where I had difficulty figuring out a method to solving it. Regardless of how difficult they were and with a little grit, perserverance (and Google), I finally came about solving those problems. I don't have a single regret about becoming a web developer, and if anything, I'm ecstatic to continue learning more about developing.
